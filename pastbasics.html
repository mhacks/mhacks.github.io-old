MHacks: Hacker Boards

Let's move past the basics!

What am I holding?

You're probably holding one of the MHacks: Refactor Hacker Boards given out at the hardware workshop! You can confirm by checking the picture to make sure that's really it.

Now that you've confirmed you've got one - or even if you don't - you're probably a) itching to find out what it is, and b) to get started hacking with it!

Getting started

Background/History

This project has been over a year in the making, having survived many different changes in fundamental goals, people on the team, and politics. Originally inspired by the awesome BoilerMake 2014 Badges, we've brought a similar idea to MHacks: a beginner-friendly, interesting piece of hardware available to everyone attending the event.

We'll have more information about the Hacker Board development process after the weekend. In the meantime, have fun playing and building with them!
